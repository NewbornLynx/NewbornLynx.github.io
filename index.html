    Volunteer Work

    Family Rescue Center

    The Family Rescue Center is a non-profit organization that provides food, clothing, toiletries, and school supplies to families in need. I began volunteering at this organization a couple of years ago to help families in need during the pandemic. After helping out for about a year, I was assigned to manually register clients into their database. To increase the efficiency of this process, I decided to developed an app for the nonprofit using Swift. From the app, a user can register to become a client, sign up to become a volunteer, and view the Family Rescue Center Website.
